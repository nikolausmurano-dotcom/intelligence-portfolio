Source Utilization Roles

The sources below are used to strengthen proof design. Unless marked as close-read elsewhere, they are not being quoted or treated as passage-level warrant.

tradecraft authority

Heuer and structured analytic technique sources govern ACH, alternatives, diagnosticity, and bias control.

judgment limits

Kahneman and Tetlock-style judgment literature governs calibration and overconfidence checks.

human-AI assurance

AI assurance sources govern when AI output should be accepted, rerun, narrowed, or blocked.
