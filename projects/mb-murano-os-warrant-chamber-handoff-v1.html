Warrant Chamber

A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

Warrant Chamber is a manuscript-centered evidence method discipline room handoff, also called The House of Evidence.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It specifies a room for preserving the relation between text, claim, source, counter-source, warrant strength, dissent, judgment, repair, revision state, and certification, with data model, workflow, screen blueprint, role-permission, event-taxonomy, design-token, SQL, GraphQL, and SVG architecture artifacts.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It matters because serious writing needs more than citations: a reviewer must be able to see what authorizes a claim, what contests it, how strong the warrant is, what version carries the judgment, and what build path would turn the handoff into a live evidentiary room.
