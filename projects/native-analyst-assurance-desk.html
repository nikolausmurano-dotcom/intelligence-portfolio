What It Is

An analyst-assurance workstation for human review of AI-supported intelligence tasks.

Source field: project blueprint public-pitch or vision field

How It Works

It makes override decisions, analyst-model disagreement, task-family calibration, source review, structured analytic technique outputs, uncertainty, assumptions, reasoning paths, scoring rules, and release chronology visible as native objects.

Source field: project blueprint mechanism field

Why It Matters

It is meant to show what human-in-the-loop review actually requires: not trust in a reviewer, but surfaces that let the reviewer inspect sources, assumptions, uncertainty, reasoning paths, and machine-aided judgments before release.
