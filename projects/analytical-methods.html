A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

A general analytical-methodology library: the place where method families, source authorities, and reusable tradecraft moves are catalogued and made checkable.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

Four mappings define every method in the catalogue: the question the method answers, the canonical source it derives from, the analytical trap it guards against, and the way the method composes with other methods.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It turns analytic methods into reusable instruments with inputs, steps, failure modes, interpretation rules, and reviewer actions.
