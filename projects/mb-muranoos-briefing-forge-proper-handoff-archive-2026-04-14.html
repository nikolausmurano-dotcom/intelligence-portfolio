What this does

Briefing Forge is a MuranoOS room handoff for turning research output into live executive briefings.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It routes research material through source grounding, audience need, judgment frame, uncertainty, dissent, BLUF, decision memo, and briefing format so briefing products remain tied to the evidence base.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It matters because briefing is not slide decoration; it is the disciplined conversion of research into decision-ready communication without losing provenance or uncertainty.
