Russia Security Desk

A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

The Russia Desk is a two-page analytical workbench: a contemporary security desk for regime security, military adaptation, influence operations, Ukraine, NATO, and strategic decision-making; and a history page for the longer imperial, Soviet, post-Soviet, and European-order inheritance that gives present judgment depth.

The mechanism is source-lane to judgment-lane conversion.

Internal workbench files, Kindle/Routledge/source library source families, journal and transcript manifests, marker packs, visual dossiers, exact-source queues, and source lineage bridges are separated by role.
