A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

Dialectic Engine is a MuranoOS cognitive-engine-room handoff for preserving narrative, philosophical, and argument-state relations.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It defines a relation grammar, synchronized view topology, interaction model, UI/UX path, MVP implementation phasing, and MuranoOS protocol so a story, chapter, or argument can be inspected through tensions, positions, transformations, and continuity rather than flattened into notes.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It matters because serious writing needs a room that preserves the structure of thought as it changes, not just a document editor.
