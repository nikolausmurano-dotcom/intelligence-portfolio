How it works

It tracks Russian reflexive-control logic through actor intent, target perception, information pathway, amplification, feedback, decision distortion, Crown Bar, Strategic Horizon, Center Oculus, Left Evidence Nave Intake, Top Crown Bar Control, Right Forge Simulation, Bottom Scriptorium Export, sample-data JSON, Forge red-cell work, and machine-readable private support file logic.
