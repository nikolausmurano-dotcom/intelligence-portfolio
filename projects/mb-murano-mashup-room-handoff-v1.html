Mashup Intelligence Room

A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

Mashup Intelligence Room is a MuranoOS room handoff for audio-source management, phrase-level extraction, compatibility discovery, and studio workflow.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It routes audio sources through an Audio Resonance Room with a SQLite/LanceDB/DuckDB three-database architecture, Resonance Engine, AI Router Console, Automation Console, ingestion, phrase extraction, compatibility search, version memory, workflow state, and export planning so creative or analytic audio work can be managed as a structured room.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It shows the broader MuranoOS pattern: a domain is not just stored, but given instruments, state, search, and workflow controls appropriate to that domain.
