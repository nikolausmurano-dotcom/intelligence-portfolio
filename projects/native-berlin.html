What this does

Berlin Occupation Zones and Refugee Crisis is a partition-and-displacement case board for studying jurisdiction, refugee pressure, intelligence risk, and early Cold War method discipline.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It routes the Berlin case through occupation zones, jurisdictional fragmentation, refugee movement, institutional stress, intelligence exposure, and analytic lessons for divided-city method discipline.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It turns a historical episode into an applied case for border method discipline, population movement, allied administration, and security risk.
