Scholarly Retrieval System - cross-disciplinary research library with a twelve-stage analytical-method discipline plane

A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

A local scholarly retrieval and evidence-review system, not merely a searchable library.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It separates the local corpus plane, retrieval plane, and twelve-stage analytical-method discipline plane: corpus admission and extraction feed sparse, dense, and semantic indexes; hybrid fusion, metadata filters, domain-aware subcorpora, reranking, and chunk-window expansion shape retrieval; query decomposition, evidence retrieval, seven-stage evidence curation, evidence scoring, analyst drafting, citation validation, claim registration, claim-gap review, follow-up query planning, contradiction review, red-team review, abstention-capable final decision, traceability, release, and source-influence tracking govern what can be said.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It matters because RAG quality depends on the whole evidence path, not only the final answer; the system makes sparse and semantic retrieval, evidence support, citation validity, abstention, contradiction, source influence, and refusal-to-answer discipline inspectable before a research claim is trusted.

Record note: professional value

Its current limit is also explicit: corpus ingestion remains the weak plane to repair before using the library as a full defensive-retrieval testbed.

Record note: professional value

What is already there

The system is live today against roughly four thousand committed books and has been growing the queryable searchable view by an order of magnitude per month, but the corpus admission pipeline is stuck — well over a hundred and forty thousand discovered records sit in a "stable" holding state, blocked by a write-lock contention pattern in the manifest that exhibits as burst-then-die behavior.

Record note: current status

Until that is resolved, the analytical method discipline plane is governing over a deliberately under-admitted library.

Record note: current status

The retrieval indices behind the live four thousand books are large and exercised — sparse and dense indices both, fused by reciprocal-rank fusion — and the analytical-method discipline discipline above them runs in production end-to-end, refusing to write past its evidence and surfacing contradictions before drawing conclusions.
