Record note: project purpose

How it works

It organizes theater-level alliance-crisis scenarios through Blue Cell, Red Cell, and White Cell roles, decision points, ambiguity traps, distraction windows, slow-fade escalation, nine-move Master Scenario Events Lists, battle-plan orders, four-hypothesis-by-ten-evidence ACH matrices, five-assumption Key Assumptions Checks, ten-dimension measurement, and post-run assessment.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It shows how strategic problems can be made inspectable through structured play, cell roles, scenario events, evidence matrices, measurement, and escalation logic rather than reduced to static policy commentary.
