AI method discipline Navigator

A concise project summary for professional review, drawn from the maintained project record.

What this does

AI method discipline Navigator is an AI-method discipline routing tool for moving from use case to risk, oversight, accountability, and assurance controls.

Record note: project purpose

How it works

It routes a proposed AI use through policy questions, risk categories, oversight duties, assurance checks, and accountability decisions so method discipline becomes a sequence of choices rather than a slogan.

Record note: working mechanism

Why it matters

It matters because AI method discipline is useful only when a reader can see which control applies to which system, risk, actor, and decision point.
