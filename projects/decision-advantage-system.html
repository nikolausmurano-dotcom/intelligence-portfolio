What is already there

Record note: current status

Every service has a contract, every ritual has a runbook, every template is filled out with a worked example, and the 90-day standup roadmap has named exit standards — by day 90 the organization must produce one Living Brief with change notes, one Counter-Brief, one Provenance Envelope, one assurance note, one Decision Log entry, and one ritual-derived architecture change.
