What This Proves

It proves that sources are being converted into project-specific mechanisms and inspection duties. It does not prove that all passages have already been read, quoted, or citation-verified. The claim ceiling remains explicit: candidate routing supports planning and proof design; passage-level citation claims require close reading and bibliographic confirmation.
