Distinctive Mechanism To Prove

The discovery side searches for transferable mechanisms from other fields and tests whether they survive context, language, timing, and analogy limits; Observatory, Scanner, Bridge, Cross-Pollination Lab, Assay Lab, Crucible, Incubator, Forge, and the Galaxy Map make those cross-field mechanism relations visible. The academic side carries problem exploration, problem statement, literature review, methodology, analysis, discussion, conclusion, and editing through stage rooms with instruments and standards; upstream changes can mark downstream stages stale, and downstream revisions can force earlier sections back into review.

Highest-Leverage Proof Upgrade

Capture a live Nexus walkthrough: start with a real research question, show cross-field mechanism discovery, then show how an upstream change marks downstream academic stages stale and forces revision.
