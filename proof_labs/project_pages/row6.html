Distinctive Mechanism To Prove

It separates the local corpus plane, retrieval plane, and twelve-stage analytical-governance plane: corpus admission and extraction feed sparse, dense, and semantic indexes; hybrid fusion, metadata filters, domain-aware subcorpora, reranking, and chunk-window expansion shape retrieval; query decomposition, evidence retrieval, seven-stage evidence curation, evidence scoring, analyst drafting, citation validation, claim registration, claim-gap review, follow-up query planning, contradiction review, red-team review, abstention-capable final decision, traceability, release, and source-influence tracking govern what can be said.
