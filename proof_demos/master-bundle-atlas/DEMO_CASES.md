# Proof Demo Cases: Master Bundle Atlas

**Project ID.** `master-bundle-atlas`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Master Bundle Atlas is an evidence-governed catalogue for 152 source, handoff, and project packets.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes each packet through artifact identity, evidence state, public claim boundary, portfolio role, and next action so a packet becomes a feature page, source substrate, implementation backlog, private support layer, or archive for stated reasons.

**Component demo cases.** 17

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.

## Flagship Demonstrations

### Master Bundle packet promotion router

**Why distinctive.** The proof must show that 152 packets are not an undifferentiated pile; each packet needs identity, extraction, authority, route, and next build decision.

**Runbook.**

- Select one packet.
- Read manifest, text-bearing state, version signal, and scope.
- Classify authority state.
- Assign promotion or archive route.
- Record next build or source-review task.

**Expected evidence.**

- Packet identity record
- Extraction state
- Authority decision
- Promotion route
- Next build task

**Acceptance criteria.**

- The packet route is justified by evidence, not by title alone.
- The public claim ceiling follows the authority state.
- Stub or low-evidence packets are blocked from inflated use.
