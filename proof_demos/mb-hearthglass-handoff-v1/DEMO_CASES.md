# Proof Demo Cases: HearthGlass

**Project ID.** `mb-hearthglass-handoff-v1`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Hearthglass is the signature Murano OS room for users "whose thinking arrives in clusters, circling motions, half-formed questions, metaphoric attractors, and unresolved tensions rather than linear finished theses." It is a thrust-room — a distinct Murano OS room class — whose job is to: (1) preserve raw corpus without flattening; (2) infer the user's latent question (the "thrust"); (3) classify.
