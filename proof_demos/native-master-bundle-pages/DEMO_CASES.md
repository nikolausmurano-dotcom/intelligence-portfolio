# Proof Demo Cases: Master Bundle Pages

**Project ID.** `native-master-bundle-pages`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** A public atlas of the Master Bundle packets, showing which archives are live inputs, authority layers, handoffs, or reference materials.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It converts a large archive set into navigable project records with title, role, implementation state, source-use implications, and next action.

**Component demo cases.** 17

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
