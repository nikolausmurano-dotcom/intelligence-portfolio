# Proof Demo Cases: MuranoOS Research Operating Environment

**Project ID.** `native-murano`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** A MuranoOS-linked manuscript command surface for coordinating the Trilogy and related human-agent work.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It connects Tauri / React / Rust / SQLite desktop architecture with rooms, workstations, agents, commands, chapter architecture, reasoning matrices, continuity checks, collision checks, and revision state so manuscript development is treated as a governed operating environment.

**Component demo cases.** 11

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.

## Flagship Demonstrations

### MuranoOS room-state operating loop

**Why distinctive.** The proof must show MuranoOS as an operating environment with rooms, commands, persistence, agents, and governance, not as a personal dashboard.

**Runbook.**

- Open or inspect a room contract.
- Invoke a room action through the UI or command bridge.
- Trace the action to command/state persistence.
- Verify that the room history records decision and next action.
- Export or block export according to claim boundary.

**Expected evidence.**

- Room contract
- Command or action record
- Persistent state record
- Decision and next-action record
- Export or public-claim boundary

**Acceptance criteria.**

- The proof shows stateful work, not just a rendered page.
- A room output can be traced to its governing contract.
- Private operating material is not exposed in public proof.
