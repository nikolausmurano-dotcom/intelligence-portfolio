# Proof Demo Cases: Scholarly Retrieval System — cross-disciplinary research library with a twelve-stage analytical-governance plane

**Project ID.** `row6`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** A local scholarly retrieval and evidence-review system, not merely a searchable library.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It separates the local corpus plane, retrieval plane, and twelve-stage analytical-governance plane: corpus admission and extraction feed sparse, dense, and semantic indexes; hybrid fusion, metadata filters, domain-aware subcorpora, reranking, and chunk-window expansion shape retrieval; query decomposition, evidence retrieval, seven-stage evidence curation, evidence scoring, analyst drafting, citation validation, claim registration, claim-gap review, follow-up query planning, contradiction review, red-team review, abstention-capable final decision, traceability, release, and source-influence.

**Component demo cases.** 10

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
