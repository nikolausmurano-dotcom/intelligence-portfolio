# Proof Demo Cases: Critical Functions Observatory — defensive UAS / critical-infrastructure research lab

**Project ID.** `critical-functions-observatory`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** A defensive UAS and critical-infrastructure research observatory built around national critical functions rather than generic drone security.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes malicious-UAS and infrastructure risk through a function-first defensive-research model: CISA National Critical Functions, FAA Remote ID, White House guidance, DOJ/DHS/FAA/FCC legal-advisory constraints, OT asset inventory, DOE/EPA-relevant function dependencies, semantic graphs, provenance vaulting, publication forge outputs, and recomputable projection logic.
