# Proof Demo Cases: Qualitative Capstone — qualitative analysis of analyst response to AI-introduced corruption

**Project ID.** `capstone-orig`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** The submitted qualitative capstone on whether structured analyst review can catch AI-introduced evidentiary corruption.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It uses fifty matched-pair scenarios, deliberately corrupted evidence packets, Structured Analytic Technique execution, sealed coding lanes, human decision review, triad reconciliation, APEX review rounds, P0/P1/P2 severity closure, G1-G5 checks, per-scenario re-coding notes, and all-fifty claim-level human validation to keep the claim bounded.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
