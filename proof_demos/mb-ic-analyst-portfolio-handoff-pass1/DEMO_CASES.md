# Proof Demo Cases: IC analyst portfolio build kit; skill taxonomy S01-S26

**Project ID.** `mb-ic-analyst-portfolio-handoff-pass1`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** IC analyst portfolio build kit; skill taxonomy S01-S26 is a preserved project packet for portfolio build kit that converts Georgetown MPAI (Master of Professional Studies in Applied Intelligence) coursework — counterterrorism papers, cyber CTI briefs, CI risk assessments, geospatial products, major academic papers — into IC-style sanitized work samples suitable for IC analyst hiring.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It converts an archive or handoff into three inspectable decisions: what the work product is for, which component pieces carry the work, and what action would turn it into a live room, paper, method, or software instrument.

**Component demo cases.** 17

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
