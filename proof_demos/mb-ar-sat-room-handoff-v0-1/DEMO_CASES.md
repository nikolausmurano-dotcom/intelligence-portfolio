**Public claim.** AR-SAT Room is a design-and-schema handoff for an adversarially robust structured-analytic-technique room.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes an intelligence problem through a Room View that binds triad method, products, and proof; Idea Refinery, Patch Forge, baseline-comparison Evaluation Harness, JSON-Schema entities, command-palette behavior, station drawer, audit timeline, evidence handling, adversarial stress, and reviewer workflow make SAT use inspectable rather than decorative.
