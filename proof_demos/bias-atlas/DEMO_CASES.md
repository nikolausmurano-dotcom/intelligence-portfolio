# Proof Demo Cases: Bias Atlas — cognitive-bias catalogue grounded in Heuer and Kahneman

**Project ID.** `bias-atlas`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** A catalogued atlas of cognitive biases that affect analytical work.

**Distinctive mechanism.** Four coordinated lenses on the bias canon — biases catalogued by analytical lane, the scholarly foundation each bias is drawn from, and the mitigation grammar that maps bias to mitigating analytical move. Reading the atlas is a sequence: identify the bias, read its foundation, follow the mitigation map.
