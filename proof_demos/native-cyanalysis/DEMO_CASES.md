# Proof Demo Cases: Cyber Analysis

**Project ID.** `native-cyanalysis`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Cyber Analysis is a cyber-intelligence method room for converting indicators and actor behavior into disciplined analytic judgment.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes technical indicators, infrastructure, observed behavior, intent, capability, confidence, and source limits through one workflow so cyber claims do not collapse into tool names or malware labels.

**Component demo cases.** 17

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
