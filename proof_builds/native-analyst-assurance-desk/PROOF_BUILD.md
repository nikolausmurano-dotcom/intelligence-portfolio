# Proof Build: Analyst Assurance Desk

**Project ID.** `native-analyst-assurance-desk`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** An analyst-assurance workstation for human review of AI-supported intelligence tasks.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It makes override decisions, analyst-model disagreement, task-family calibration, source review, structured analytic technique outputs, uncertainty, assumptions, reasoning paths, scoring rules, and release chronology visible as native objects.

**Use value.** It is meant to show what human-in-the-loop review actually requires: not trust in a reviewer, but surfaces that let the reviewer inspect sources, assumptions, uncertainty, reasoning paths, and machine-aided judgments before release.

**Minimum public demo.** Trace one Russia claim through Task And Method Assignment; confirm source lane, analytic variable, confidence boundary, and policy-facing output.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
