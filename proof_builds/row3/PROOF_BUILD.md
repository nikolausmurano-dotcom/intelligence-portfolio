# Proof Build: AI Extraction Defense — five-layer architecture protecting proprietary AI models from being extracted, copied, or distilled by adversaries

**Project ID.** `row3`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** An extraction-defense architecture for protecting valuable analytical models from copying, distillation, or downstream imitation.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It treats model extraction as a counterintelligence problem: L1 behavioral shaping, L2 dynamic controls, L3 canary narratives, L4 tarpit/degraded-environment design, access segmentation, query-behavior signals, output fingerprinting, latency injection, logical loops, and post-hoc extraction detection work together.

**Use value.** The distinctive move is that defense continues after the interaction; a canary can survive paraphrase, while tarpit and ATLAS-style threat-model thinking make extraction costly, observable, and strategically misleading.
