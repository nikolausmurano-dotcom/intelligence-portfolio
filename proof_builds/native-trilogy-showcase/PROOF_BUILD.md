# Proof Build: Book Trilogy Showcase

**Project ID.** `native-trilogy-showcase`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** Book Trilogy Showcase is the reader-facing exhibit for a governed three-book manuscript system.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It presents the trilogy through public book order, canonical IDs, seventy-two chapter profiles, ReaderEdition targets, FinalProof argument backbone, ConstructionKits, PassageTargets, Democratic Dialectic voice material, book-specific registers, chapter shape, bridge logic, dependency logic, terminology controls, visual identity, and rewrite-state limits.

**Use value.** It matters because a reader should see what the books are arguing and how the chapter system is governed, while also seeing that final public prose admission remains unfinished rather than completed.

**Minimum public demo.** Apply Canonical Chapter Identity to one chapter dossier; confirm title, visual, source state, rewrite target, and handoff decision.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
