# Proof Build: Development Process — how projects move from idea to built, reviewed work

**Project ID.** `planning`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** A public explanation of how projects are organized and developed across the portfolio.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It maps the route from idea generation to source gathering, project room creation, instrument selection, review, release, and status tracking.

**Use value.** It helps a first-time reader understand that the site is not a pile of pages but a project-development system with repeatable stages.

**Minimum public demo.** Test one representative case; confirm input (policy problem, governance failure, oversight gap...) produces output (specific problem the architecture is meant to govern) and leaves a visible decision or artifact.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
