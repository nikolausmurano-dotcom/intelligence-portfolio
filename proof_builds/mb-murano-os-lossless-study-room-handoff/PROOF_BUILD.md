# Proof Build: Lossless Study Room

**Project ID.** `mb-murano-os-lossless-study-room-handoff`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Lossless Study Room is a MuranoOS handoff for Applied Intelligence Mission Control.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It combines distraction capture, per-course and cross-course Obsidian-style Zettelkasten structure, flashcard generation, source capture, coursework state, and review surfaces so graduate study becomes a governed knowledge-production room instead of scattered notes.

**Use value.** It matters because the same machinery that preserves coursework can later preserve research decisions, sources, and project continuity.

**Minimum public demo.** Open or inspect the local runtime path for Lossless Study Room Handoff Identity; confirm a room, action, or state record traces to the governing contract.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
