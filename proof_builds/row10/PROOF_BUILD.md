# Proof Build: Analyst Workstation — eight-page interactive AI-augmented intelligence-analysis handbook

**Project ID.** `row10`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** An analyst workstation for AI-augmented intelligence analysis.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It converts AI support into inspection surfaces: knowledge production, prompt engineering discipline, cognitive-bias safeguards, Key Assumptions Check, live SAT visualization instruments, source review, ICD/NIST-bound assurance language, analyst-model disagreement, and human acceptance or correction before conclusions move forward. The cross-page workstation can connect AI Governance Framework Navigator, EU AI Act considerations, AI System Assessment, AI Failure Mode Taxonomy, OSINT Narrative Mapping, OSIRIS LLM, and per-stage AI/HITL/ICD/NIST checks.

**Use value.** It matters because human-in-the-loop review only has value when the human has a concrete workstation for inspecting sources, uncertainty, assumptions, structured analytic technique outputs, and reasoning paths rather than a generic prompt window.

**Minimum public demo.** run one analytic question and show the AI output, human correction, SAT trace, and confidence boundary

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.

## Flagship Proof Builds

### Human-AI structured-analysis delta proof

- Claim: The analyst workstation should show where AI helps, where it fails, and where human structured analysis changes the result.
- Input: AI output, human analyst judgment, evidence ledger, SAT method, and confidence rule
- Operation: compare AI and human judgments through method assignment, evidence/assumption separation, ACH or alternative review, and confidence gate
- Expected output: human-AI delta record with accept, qualify, block, rerun, or quarantine decision
- Verification check: run one analytic question and show the AI output, human correction, SAT trace, and confidence boundary
