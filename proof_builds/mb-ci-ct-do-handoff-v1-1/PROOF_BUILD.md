# Proof Build: CI/CT DO tradecraft v1.1; SAT enumeration + 12 analyst templates (BLUF, ACH, ICD-203)

**Project ID.** `mb-ci-ct-do-handoff-v1-1`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** CI/CT DO tradecraft v1.1 is an unclassified method packet for converting a long-form counterterrorism and counterintelligence paper into analyst-ready tradecraft templates.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It combines Red Team, kill-chain, Diamond Model, ACH, ICD-203 language discipline, BLUF forms, and twelve analyst templates so operational tradecraft, analytic method, and public-safe portfolio framing remain distinct.

**Use value.** It matters because CI/CT material has to be useful without becoming sensitive: the packet shows how to turn doctrine into professional templates while preserving source, role, and release boundaries.

**Minimum public demo.** Run one analytic question through CI/CT DO tradecraft v1.1; SAT enumeration + 12 analyst templates (BLUF, ACH, ICD-203) Handoff Identity; confirm evidence, assumptions, method decision, confidence limit, and human/AI delta.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
