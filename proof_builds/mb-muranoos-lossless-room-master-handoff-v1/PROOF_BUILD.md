# Proof Build: Room Master (constitution)

**Project ID.** `mb-muranoos-lossless-room-master-handoff-v1`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Room Master is the MuranoOS constitution for what a room is allowed to be.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It defines room purpose, UI/UX expectations, live state, memory, instruments, export surfaces, and Red Team lossless replacement discipline so future rooms inherit a common architecture instead of improvising their own rules.

**Use value.** It matters because MuranoOS can only scale if each room preserves evidence, roles, state, and review obligations in a consistent way.

**Minimum public demo.** Open or inspect the local runtime path for Room Master (constitution) Handoff Identity; confirm a room, action, or state record traces to the governing contract.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
