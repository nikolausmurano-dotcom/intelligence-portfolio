# Proof Build: Wargaming — theater-level alliance-crisis wargame workbench

**Project ID.** `wargaming`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** A wargaming and crisis-analysis portfolio surface.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It organizes theater-level alliance-crisis scenarios through Blue Cell, Red Cell, and White Cell roles, decision points, ambiguity traps, distraction windows, slow-fade escalation, nine-move Master Scenario Events Lists, battle-plan orders, four-hypothesis-by-ten-evidence ACH matrices, five-assumption Key Assumptions Checks, ten-dimension measurement, and post-run assessment.

**Use value.** It shows how strategic problems can be made inspectable through structured play, cell roles, scenario events, evidence matrices, measurement, and escalation logic rather than reduced to static policy commentary.
