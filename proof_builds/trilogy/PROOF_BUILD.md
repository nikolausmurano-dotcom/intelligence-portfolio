# Proof Build: Book Trilogy — Security, Dialectic, Repair : a three-book governed arc on dangerous power, democratic formation, and political repair after harm (the full manuscript corpus across 72 chapters)

**Project ID.** `trilogy`

**Proof tier.** specified-proof-plan-needs-demo

**Public claim.** A governed three-book manuscript system, not a dashboard of chapter counts.

**Distinctive mechanism.** The trilogy is organized across restraint, democratic formation, and repair. ReaderEdition chapters provide the public rewrite target; FinalProof preserves argument backbone; ConstructionKits govern chapter shape; PassageTargets supply sentence pressure and source-bearing formulations; the Master Implementation Workbook governs bridge, dependency, and terminology logic; Democratic Dialectic supplies selective voice material, especially for Public Book II.

**Use value.** It matters because it shows long-form argument governance: seventy-two chapters are being managed as an intellectual architecture with source scaffolds, book-specific registers, and visual identity rather than as isolated essays.

**Minimum public demo.** show one chapter dossier from input stack to rewrite decision, including why the title and visual belong only to that page

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
