# Proof Build: Warrant Chamber

**Project ID.** `mb-murano-os-warrant-chamber-handoff-v1`

**Proof tier.** source-backed-proof-build

**Public claim.** Warrant Chamber is a manuscript-centered evidence governance room handoff, also called The House of Evidence.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It specifies a room for preserving the relation between text, claim, source, counter-source, warrant strength, dissent, judgment, repair, revision state, and certification, with data model, workflow, screen blueprint, role-permission, event-taxonomy, design-token, SQL, GraphQL, and SVG architecture artifacts.

**Use value.** It matters because serious writing needs more than citations: a reviewer must be able to see what authorizes a claim, what contests it, how strong the warrant is, what version carries the judgment, and what build path would turn the handoff into a live evidentiary room.

**Minimum public demo.** Open or inspect the local runtime path for Warrant Chamber Handoff Identity; confirm a room, action, or state record traces to the governing contract.

**Claim ceiling.** The page may claim proof-structured components and visible verification targets. It may not claim external validation, production deployment, or completed empirical findings unless the project-specific record states those facts.
