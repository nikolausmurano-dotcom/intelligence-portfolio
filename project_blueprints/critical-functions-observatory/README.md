# Critical Functions Observatory — defensive UAS / critical-infrastructure research lab

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** A defensive UAS and critical-infrastructure research observatory built around national critical functions rather than generic drone security.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes malicious-UAS and infrastructure risk through a function-first defensive-research model: CISA National Critical Functions, FAA Remote ID, White House guidance, DOJ/DHS/FAA/FCC legal-advisory constraints, OT asset inventory, DOE/EPA-relevant function dependencies, semantic graphs, provenance vaulting, publication forge outputs, and recomputable projection logic. It also guards against turning defensive analysis into operational attack guidance by filtering timing, payload, and bypass details.

**Why it matters.** It matters because critical-infrastructure defense should begin with the function being protected, the legal and operational constraints around intervention, the projection engine that can be recomputed, and the evidence path that supports each protective claim.
