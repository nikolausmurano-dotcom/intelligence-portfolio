# Culinary Atelier

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** Culinary Atelier is a MuranoOS boundary-arbitration packet for food knowledge, domestic logistics, culture, and service.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It separates Federated Studio, Service, Domestic Logistics, Culture, recipe knowledge, ingredient state, intent, red-team review, and executable meal planning so adjacent private pages are consolidated into one professional operating room without exposing private content.

**Why it matters.** It matters as a MuranoOS design example: even non-academic domains can be organized into sources, constraints, workflows, review, and execution.
