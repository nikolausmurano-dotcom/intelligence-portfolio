# Bias Atlas — cognitive-bias catalogue grounded in Heuer and Kahneman

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** A catalogued atlas of cognitive biases that affect analytical work.

**Distinctive mechanism.** Four coordinated lenses on the bias canon — biases catalogued by analytical lane, the scholarly foundation each bias is drawn from, and the mitigation grammar that maps bias to mitigating analytical move. Reading the atlas is a sequence: identify the bias, read its foundation, follow the mitigation map.

**Why it matters.** It shows the utility of Bias Atlas — cognitive-bias catalogue grounded in Heuer and Kahneman by tying the project purpose to the mechanism that makes it inspectable, reusable, or professionally valuable.

Open work: the binding from each catalogued bias to its specific admitted Heuer or Kahneman passage has not yet been wired through the source-register pipeline. ### Source use
