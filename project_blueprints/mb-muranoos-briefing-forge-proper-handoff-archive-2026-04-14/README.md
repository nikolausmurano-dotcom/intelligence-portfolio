# Briefing Forge

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** Briefing Forge is a MuranoOS room handoff for turning research output into live executive briefings.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes research material through source grounding, audience need, judgment frame, uncertainty, dissent, BLUF, decision memo, and briefing format so briefing products remain tied to the evidence base.

**Why it matters.** It matters because briefing is not slide decoration; it is the disciplined conversion of research into decision-ready communication without losing provenance or uncertainty.
