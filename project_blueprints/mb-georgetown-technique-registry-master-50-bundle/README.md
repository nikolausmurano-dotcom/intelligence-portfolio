# 50-course Georgetown SSP registry; 7,500+ technique lines; capstone plug tags [SR/EP/IR/SC/G]

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** Georgetown technique registry is a method-authority packet covering all 50 SSP courses and thousands of course-derived technique lines.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It preserves U.S. National Security Policy through Intelligence and War, including the Research-Methods, Statistics, SAT, Operations Research, Game-Theory, Disruptive-Analytics, Forecasting, Modeling-Complexity, Political-Analyst, and Writing-for-Intel spine; old-to-new SEST crosswalks, Scenario Registry tags, Evidence Packet tags, Interagency Roles, Scoring, and Gates connect the registry to capstone and future method work.

**Why it matters.** It matters because a research platform needs a deep technique inventory before it can recommend, combine, or test methods responsibly.
