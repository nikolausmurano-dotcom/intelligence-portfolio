# CI/CT DO tradecraft v1.1; SAT enumeration + 12 analyst templates (BLUF, ACH, ICD-203)

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** CI/CT DO tradecraft v1.1 is an unclassified method packet for converting a long-form counterterrorism and counterintelligence paper into analyst-ready tradecraft templates.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It combines Red Team, kill-chain, Diamond Model, ACH, ICD-203 language discipline, BLUF forms, and twelve analyst templates so operational tradecraft, analytic method, and public-safe portfolio framing remain distinct.

**Why it matters.** It matters because CI/CT material has to be useful without becoming sensitive: the packet shows how to turn doctrine into professional templates while preserving source, role, and release boundaries.
