# Historical Studies — eleven historical and operational case studies, including the manuscript Trilogy

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** A historical-studies index for manuscript, diplomacy, war theory, international law, peacebuilding, and operational case pages.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It distinguishes long-form scholarship, international-affairs historical analysis, and operational simulations instead of presenting all history-facing work as one kind of object.

**Why it matters.** It makes the portfolio legible as applied historical reasoning: some pages are manuscripts, some are legal or diplomatic analyses, and some are scenario-based operational case studies.

A historical-studies index for manuscript, diplomacy, war theory, international law, peacebuilding, and operational case pages.

The operational-simulation pages (Habitat, Pharm, Respite, Uscri) are complete case studies in their own right. The international-affairs pages (Cicc, Peace) carry substantial reference content.
