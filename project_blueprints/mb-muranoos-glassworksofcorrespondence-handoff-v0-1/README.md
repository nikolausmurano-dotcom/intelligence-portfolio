# Glassworks of Correspondence

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** Glassworks of Correspondence is a MuranoOS room handoff for managing metaphor, allegory, correspondence, and interpretive transfer.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It routes a metaphor or allegorical pattern through source, target, transfer logic, distortion risk, interpretive limit, and reuse decision so symbolic language remains governed rather than ornamental.

**Why it matters.** It matters because sophisticated writing and analysis often depend on transfer; this room makes the transfer conditions and risks visible.
