# Reflexive Control Chamber — Russian reflexive-control & strategic-deception research chamber

## Public Pitch

**What it is.** A reflexive-control analysis chamber for influence, perception management, and adversarial shaping of judgment.

**Distinctive mechanism.** It tracks Russian reflexive-control logic through actor intent, target perception, information pathway, amplification, feedback, decision distortion, Crown Bar, Strategic Horizon, Center Oculus, Left Evidence Nave Intake, Top Crown Bar Control, Right Forge Simulation, Bottom Scriptorium Export, sample-data JSON, Forge red-cell work, and machine-readable API/event/UI-state/release-gate logic.

**Why it matters.** It makes influence analysis operational: not merely who said what, but how perception is shaped into action and how a chamber could preserve that reasoning for review, simulation, and export.
